Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how RolePrep collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our website, interview practice tools, payment flow, and support channels.

Last Updated: April 9, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use RolePrep, we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account and session information such as your user ID, role preferences, selected plan, and session activity.
  • Interview content such as job descriptions, resume notes, uploaded resume context, audio responses, transcripts, and analysis results.
  • Technical information such as browser type, device characteristics, app install state, and general usage data needed to operate the product.
  • Payment-related information associated with purchases, including plan type and payment status. Payment processing is handled through third-party providers such as Razorpay, and we do not store full card details on our servers.
  • Support communications you send to us through email or other support channels.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide interview practice sessions, scoring, transcript generation, and related product features.
  • To create, maintain, and resume your interview sessions across devices and visits.
  • To process purchases, manage plan access, session credits, and premium features.
  • To improve product quality, diagnose issues, prevent abuse, and maintain platform security.
  • To respond to support requests, billing questions, and technical problems.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our policies.

3. Audio, Resume, and Interview Data

RolePrep is built around interview preparation. That means information you submit for practice, including audio recordings, transcripts, job descriptions, resume context, and generated feedback, may be processed to deliver the service.

Please do not upload highly sensitive personal information unless it is necessary for your interview preparation.

4. Payments

Paid plans and session credits may be processed through third-party payment providers, including Razorpay. When you make a purchase, certain transaction-related information may be shared with the payment provider to complete the transaction and confirm payment status.

Payment confirmation and plan activation may also rely on secure webhook events sent by the payment processor.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in limited circumstances, such as:

  • With service providers who help us host, secure, analyze, or operate the platform.
  • With payment providers to process transactions and verify payment events.
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or valid governmental request.
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of RolePrep, our users, or others.

6. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, maintain account/session continuity, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards intended to protect information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Choices

  • You may choose not to submit optional information, though some features may not function fully without it.
  • You may contact us regarding support, billing questions, or privacy-related requests.
  • You may stop using the service at any time.

9. Children’s Privacy

RolePrep is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or need support, please contact: support@roleprep.in
