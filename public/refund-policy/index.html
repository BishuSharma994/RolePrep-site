Refund Policy

This Refund Policy explains how payments for RolePrep digital products, plans, and credits are handled.

Last Updated: April 9, 2026

1. Digital Product Policy

RolePrep provides digital services, including interview sessions, plan access, credits, analysis features, and related online functionality. Because these are digital products and services, all purchases are generally final.

2. No Refunds

All payments made to RolePrep are non-refundable, except where a refund is required under applicable law.

Once a payment is successfully made for a digital plan, credit, or premium access, we do not provide refunds, partial refunds, or exchanges simply because the product was not used, was partially used, or the user changed their mind.

3. Billing Issues

If you believe you were charged incorrectly, charged more than once, or experienced a payment-processing issue, please contact us and we will review the matter. Review of a billing issue does not guarantee a refund, but we will investigate genuine payment errors.

4. Failed or Incomplete Payments

If a payment fails, is interrupted, or is not confirmed by the payment provider, access or credits may not be activated until the payment is successfully verified. Payment activation may depend on provider confirmation and webhook processing.

5. Chargebacks and Disputes

If you initiate a chargeback or payment dispute, we reserve the right to review the associated account, suspend access, or limit further use of paid services while the dispute is being resolved.

6. Contact

For billing support or questions about this Refund Policy, contact: support@roleprep.in
